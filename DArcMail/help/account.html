Searching for an account

  • You can search on a partial or complete account name.
  • The search string you supply will be automatically wild-carded at the beginning and end.
  • Searches are case-insensitive.
  • If you do not supply any search string, the search will match all accounts.

Account info page

The account info page has three components:

  1. The "Set Account" button. Pressing this button will set the current account for any subsequent message or address search (until a new current account is set). In addition, the results of any previous search still visible under the "Results" tab will be deleted.
  2. Account attributes.
  3. A list of folders in the account.
